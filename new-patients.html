Your First Visit, Step by Step

We do things a little differently here — and patients love it. Here's how your first appointment at ProHealth Chiropractic in Garden City, Kansas typically flows.

01

Warm Welcome

Tonya will greet you at the front desk and get you settled. If you haven't already completed your new patient paperwork, we'll get that taken care of quickly and comfortably.

02

Health History & Consultation

Dr. Yager sits down with you — not standing with a clipboard, but actually sitting down — to listen. He'll ask about your history, your current symptoms, your goals, and anything else that matters to your health.

03

ProAdjuster Assessment

The ProAdjuster technology scans your spine, measuring exactly how each segment is responding. This computer-generated analysis tells Dr. Yager precisely where your spine needs attention — no guessing involved.

04

Your First Adjustment

Using the findings from your ProAdjuster scan, Dr. Yager performs a personalized adjustment — gentle, precise, and completely pain-free. Many patients notice relief the same day. Some say the same hour.

05

Care Plan Discussion

Dr. Yager will explain what he found and what a realistic care plan looks like for you. No pressure — just honest information so you can make the best decision for your body and your life.

06

Ongoing Support

You'll leave knowing exactly what to expect and when to come back. The team will check in with you and ensure every follow-up visit builds on the last — steady progress, not just temporary relief.

Common Questions

New Patient FAQ

Do I need a referral to see a chiropractor in Kansas?

No referral is needed. You can contact us directly and schedule your first appointment without needing to go through your primary care physician first. Kansas allows patients to seek chiropractic care directly.

Does insurance cover chiropractic care at ProHealth?

Many insurance plans cover chiropractic care. We recommend calling your insurer to confirm your benefits before your visit. Our team can also help you understand your coverage. We work with most major insurance providers — call us at (620) 271-0243 for specifics.

Why is there a deposit required for new patients?

We reserve a significant block of time for every new patient — much more than a standard visit — so Dr. Yager can properly evaluate your needs. The deposit ensures that time is protected for you and not lost if you need to reschedule. It is applied toward your first visit.

What is the ProAdjuster and does it hurt?

The ProAdjuster is a computer-guided chiropractic instrument that measures spinal resonance and delivers gentle, precise adjustments. It does not involve twisting or cracking — most patients describe the sensation as a gentle tapping. It is completely comfortable, even for people who have been nervous about chiropractic care.

How long is a first appointment?

Your first visit typically takes 45–60 minutes. This includes your health consultation, ProAdjuster assessment, first adjustment, and a discussion of your care plan. Follow-up visits are much faster — usually 20–30 minutes.

Can children be treated at ProHealth Chiropractic?

Absolutely. Dr. Yager has treated patients of all ages — including newborns, children, teenagers, and seniors. The ProAdjuster is especially well-suited for pediatric patients because of its gentle approach.

What should I wear to my appointment?

Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing. You don't need to bring anything special — just yourself and your completed intake forms if you've filled them out ahead of time.
