## On-Page SEO Requirements (Per Page)

- **Word count:** 800–1,200 words per condition/service page
- **H1:** Must include city + keyword (e.g., "Back Pain Chiropractor in Garden City, KS")
- **H2/H3 structure:** Break content into scannable sections
- **Embedded Google Map** on Contact and homepage
- **Clear CTA** above the fold and at the bottom of every page (phone number + appointment button)
- **Schema markup:**
  - Homepage/Contact: `LocalBusiness` + `MedicalBusiness`
  - Condition pages: `MedicalCondition`
  - Blog posts: `Article`
- Use both "Garden City, KS" and "Garden City, Kansas" naturally throughout — don't use only one form

## Google Business Profile (32% of Local Pack Ranking)

GBP is the single highest-leverage action for local rankings.

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Primary category: **Chiropractor**
- [ ] Secondary categories: Acupuncture, Pain Management Physician, Sports Medicine Physician
- [ ] All services listed with descriptions
- [ ] Hours accurate and complete
- [ ] 20+ photos (interior, staff, equipment, exterior, treatment rooms)
- [ ] Weekly GBP posts (promotions, tips, new patient specials)
- [ ] Build a review acquisition system — recency and quantity beat competitors with older reviews
- [ ] Answer every review (positive and negative)
- [ ] Q&A section populated with common patient questions
