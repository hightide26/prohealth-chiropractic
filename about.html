Your Chiropractor in Garden City, KS

Dr. James Yager, D.C.

Dr. Yager has been serving the Garden City, Kansas community since 1999 — the year he graduated from Parker University in Dallas, TX, one of the nation's most respected chiropractic institutions.

From the very beginning, Dr. Yager's approach has been simple: find the root cause of your pain instead of just managing symptoms. That philosophy, combined with the precision of the ProAdjuster technology, has made ProHealth Chiropractic the preferred choice for patients across Garden City and the surrounding region.

What patients notice first isn't the equipment — it's the doctor. Dr. Yager takes time to truly understand your history, listen to your concerns, and explain exactly what's happening in your body. He's consistently described by patients as one of the most caring and thorough doctors they've ever seen.
