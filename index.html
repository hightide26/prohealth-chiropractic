The ProAdjuster — Chiropractic Reimagined

Most people avoid the chiropractor because they're afraid of the "pop and crack." With the ProAdjuster, that's never an issue. This FDA-cleared, computer-guided instrument reads your spine like a diagnostic tool and delivers precise, gentle oscillation pulses exactly where your body needs them.

No Twisting or Cracking Completely comfortable — even for first-timers and those with fear of manual adjustments
Near-Immediate Results Most patients notice relief from the very first visit — not weeks later
