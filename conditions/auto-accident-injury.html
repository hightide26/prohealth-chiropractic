Common Auto Accident Injuries We Treat

The most common injuries seen after a car accident in Garden City, Kansas include:

  • Whiplash — Rapid back-and-forth movement of the neck that strains muscles, ligaments, and cervical joints. Often the #1 complaint after rear-end collisions.
  • Neck pain & stiffness — Reduced range of motion, aching, and radiating pain into the shoulders or arms.
  • Back pain — Mid or lower back pain from compressed or herniated discs and sprained muscles.
  • Herniated discs — Discs forced out of alignment by sudden impact, causing nerve compression and radiating pain.
  • Headaches & migraines — Post-accident headaches often originate from cervical spine dysfunction, not head trauma.
  • Shoulder pain — From seatbelt force, airbag impact, or bracing the steering wheel at impact.
  • Soft tissue injuries — Sprains and strains throughout the body that may not appear on X-rays but cause significant pain and limited motion.

Does Insurance Cover Chiropractic After a Car Accident?

In most cases, yes. If you were involved in a car accident in Kansas, your treatment may be covered through:

  • PIP (Personal Injury Protection) — Kansas is a no-fault state, so your own auto insurance PIP coverage typically pays first, regardless of who caused the accident.
  • Third-party liability — If the other driver was at fault, their insurance may be responsible for your medical bills.
  • Health insurance — Your personal health plan may cover chiropractic care as well.
  • Attorney-referred / lien-based treatment — If you're working with a personal injury attorney, treatment may be provided on a lien basis, meaning you pay nothing until your case settles.
